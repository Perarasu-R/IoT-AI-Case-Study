# IoT & Artificial Intelligence Integration – Case Study

## Project Overview
This project presents a case study on the integration of the Internet of Things (IoT) and Artificial Intelligence (AI). It explains how AI enhances IoT systems, their real-world applications, benefits, challenges, and future scope.

## Objective
To study how IoT and Artificial Intelligence work together to build intelligent and automated systems.
